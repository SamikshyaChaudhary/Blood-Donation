✓ You CAN Donate If...

  • ✓ Age 18–65 years old
  • ✓ Weight at least 50 kg
  • ✓ In good general health today
  • ✓ Hemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL (F), ≥ 13.5 (M)
  • ✓ 3+ months since last donation
  • ✓ No alcohol in last 24 hours
  • ✓ Adequate sleep (≥ 6 hours)

✕ Temporary Deferrals

  • ✕ Active fever or infection
  • ✕ Recent surgery (6 months)
  • ✕ Pregnancy or postpartum (6 months)
  • ✕ HIV, Hepatitis B/C
  • ✕ Tattoo or piercing (6 months)
  • ✕ On antibiotics or blood thinners
  • ✕ Recent malaria-risk travel (3 months)
