Who We Are

JeevanConnect was founded in 2024 by students and health advocates in Lumbini Province to solve the blood shortage problem in local hospitals.

Our platform is completely free and connects voluntary donors with patients who urgently need blood — verified, fast, and reliable.

Our Mission

No one should lose their life because blood was unavailable. We build real-time, trusted donor networks across Nepal.

We also run community awareness drives, school campaigns, and work with local NGOs to promote voluntary blood donation.

Our Core Values

❤️ Compassion

Every decision is guided by empathy for patients and families in need.

🛡️ Trust & Safety

All donors are verified. Privacy and data confidentiality are non-negotiable.

🌐 Community

Built by and for the community of Nepal, not for profit.

⚡ Speed

In emergencies every minute matters. Instant donor-patient connection.

🤝 Inclusivity

Anyone eligible can donate. All communities and blood groups welcome.

🔍 Transparency

No hidden steps. Donors see requests, recipients see availability.
